How long have I photographed and what got me into it?

I have always been artistic and felt most myself in the nature. I have been hikig for couple of years now, and I have constantly been amazed by our beautiful nature. I started my photography journey by capturing the views with my phone while hiking. Slowly I got more interested in editing, and paying more attention to the techinal side of photographing. I find photographing and editing highly therapeutical during a hectic and stressfull time in my life. A year ago I bought my first camera and since then I have been spending my freetime capturing beautiful scenes and moments
